Boris The Animal

Boris has an insect-like pet named the Weasel,
who lives in his hollow right hand, serving
as Boris' main source of spikes to kill

  • Alternative Name: The Animal
  • Special Abiities: Time Jumping
  • Specie: Bolognite
  • Temper: Hot
Boris The Animal is the main antagonist
of the third film in the franchise,
titled Men in Black III. Boris is an
intergalactic criminal and the last
known member of the Boglodite race.
